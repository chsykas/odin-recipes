Stuffed Peppers

Recipe Description

Filled are foods of many peoples that usually consist of vegetables and a filling of rice (many call them "orphans") or meat or a mixture of the two ("married").

They are mainly cooked in the summer, as at that time the vegetables used ripen, and because they are light food they can be eaten comfortably in hot weather, as a hot or cold dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 green peppers
  • 2 yellow peppers
  • 4 soup spoons olive oil
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 200gr basmati rice
  • 200gr tomato concache
  • 100gr cheddar grated

For serving

  • 2 soup spoons parsley chopped

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C in the air.
  2. Cut the cap off the peppers, approximately 1 cm below the stem.
  3. Remove the seeds and put the peppers in a 20x20 cm pan.
  4. Brush with half the olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Heat the remaining olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  6. Chop the onion and garlic.
  7. Sauté the onion and garlic for 2-3 minutes until they soften slightly.
  8. Add the rice and mix with a spoon.
  9. Add the concache tomato, salt and pepper. Stir and remove the pan from the heat.
  10. Divide the mixture among the peppers, sprinkle with the grated cheddar and cover the pan with aluminum foil.
  11. Bake in the oven for 15-20 minutes.
  12. Remove the aluminum foil and bake for another 10 minutes.
  13. Remove the pan from the oven, sprinkle with the chopped parsley, and serve.
